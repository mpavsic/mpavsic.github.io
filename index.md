I am Miha Pavšič, researcher and teaching assistant at [University of Ljubljana](https://www.uni-lj.si/), [Faculty of Chemistry and Chemical Technology](https://www.fkkt.uni-lj.si/). To describe my work in one sentence: closely looking at big complex molecules in cells, trying to decipher how they work, and using gained skills and experience to catalyze transfer of knowledge to young generation of future scientists. *Sounds nice, ha?* In fact it is - both [research](research) and [teaching](teaching), going hand in hand, bring a lot of excitement and fulfillment to my life, further topped with exploration of the nature at a much bigger scale - by cycling and mountain hiking.
