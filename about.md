### Miha Pavšič

Geo-info
: Born in [Maribor](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maribor), living near [Ljubljana](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ljubljana), [Slovenia](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Slovenia).

Title
: *Scientific*: **Ph.D.** in Biochemistry and Molecular Biology
: *Academic*: **Assistant Professor**, field of Biochemistry and Molecular Biology

Education
: After finishing secondary school [II. Gimnazija Maribor](https://www.druga.si/en/), I moved to Ljubljana to study **Biochemistry** at the [Faculty of Chemistry and Chemical Technology](https://www.fkkt.uni-lj.si/) ([University of Ljubljana](https://www.uni-lj.si/)).
: Following graduation I finished a Ph.D. study of **Biomedicine** (scientific field of *Biochemistry and Molecular Biology*) at the [Faculty of Medicine](https://www.mf.uni-lj.si/) at the same university.

Work
: During my Ph.D. study, I worked as a young researcher at [Jožef Stefan Institute](https://www.ijs.si), [Department of Biochemistry, Molecular and Structural Biology](https://www.ijs.si/ijsw/Biochemistry%20and%20Molecular%20Biology%20B1).
: Currently, I'm employed as a **researcher**, **assistant** and **teacher** at the [Faculty of Chemistry and Chemical Technology](https://www.fkkt.uni-lj.si/) at the [University of Ljubljana](https://www.uni-lj.si/).
: *Please, see [Research](research) and [Teaching](teaching) sections for more details.*
